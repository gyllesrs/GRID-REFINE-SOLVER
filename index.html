About

GRS (Grid Refine Solver) is a free tool developed to facilitate the generation of two-dimensional structured grids with an intuitive user interface and options for edge refinement and interpolation.

GRS enables the quick creation of geometries from images and the precise definition of perimeters using linear interpolation or cubic splines.

With a grid generator based on elliptic differential equations, GRS ensures smooth and high-quality grids, which are essential for accurate numerical simulations.

The tool offers export of grids in simple, cmesh, and msh formats, which are compatible with commercial software such as Fluent, and includes various metrics to evaluate grid quality.

With detailed controls on numerical resolution, GRS aids in studies of convergence and mesh refinement independence and represents a significant advance in democratizing access to grid generation tools, benefiting students and engineers.
